FAQ

Do you have a question about SolveXchange that you want
answered? Check out some Frequently Asked Questions that
students have commonly asked below.

Competition Specifics

What exactly do I have to create?

You’ll be creating a pitch for an idea you’re passionate about. It doesn’t have to be a full business — just something innovative that you want to share with others.

Do I have to pitch a business?

Not at all! Your pitch can be a product, service, project, or initiative — as long as it’s creative and impactful.

Can I compete in groups?

Yes! You can team up with up to four people. Working with friends can make the process even more fun.

Do I need to be part of a chapter at my school to compete?

Nope — anyone can join! You can sign up individually or with a group, submit your idea, and present it at the pitch competition. No chapters needed.

When do I have to submit my proposal?

The proposal deadline will be on May 20th, 2026. The pitch competition will take place in the spring of 2026, but the exact date has yet to be determined.

How do I know if I’m eligible to compete?

If you’re a secondary school student in Canada, you’re more than welcome to participate!

Funding

Will we get funding?

Yes! Winners won’t just be recognized — they’ll receive funding to make their idea a reality and create lasting impact.

What if my idea needs more than the budget?

No problem! While we do want you to consider the viability aspect of your plan, we also want all participants to think big! If you find that your idea is ambitious and the costs exceed the budget, don't let this deter you! While we may not be able to fully fund your project, we are able to explore alternative avenues to help bring your idea to life. This includes arranging meetings with industry professionals, providing social media coverage, and assisting you in securing personal sponsors!

Benefits and Recognition

Why should I join, and how will this benefit me?

Present your pitch at our nationwide competition for the opportunity to secure funding and turn your idea into reality. The winner will not only receive financial support but also be recognized across Canada for their endeavors. Furthermore, there's a chance to be featured on our website and social media.

By participating, you'll collaborate with like-minded individuals, forge new connections, and experience the meaningful impact of initiating change in your community.

Can I use this as something to add to my post-secondary applications?

Of course! Certainly! SolveXchange offers a valuable opportunity to learn and showcase your entrepreneurial skills, initiative, community action, collaboration and creativity. Participating in our competitions can provide a rich experience to enhance your applications. Feel free to connect with us on LinkedIn and add us to your profile!
