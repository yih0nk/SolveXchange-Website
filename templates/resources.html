SOLVEXCHANGE PITCH CONTEST RUBRIC

Lesson 1

The Importance of a Problem

This lesson shows that strong ideas begin with identifying meaningful problems and generating many possible solutions. Using creativity tools helps turn everyday challenges into impactful ideas.

Lesson 2

Problem Deep Dive & Community Validation

This lesson focuses on deeply understanding a problem before developing solutions. Key concepts include distinguishing between symptoms and root causes, identifying affected groups, validating problems through research and surveys, and forming clear problem statements.

Lesson 3

DESIGNING A HIGH-QUALITY, INNOVATIVE SOLUTION

This lesson shows how to turn an idea into a clear, well-structured solution that directly addresses a problem. It emphasizes defining how the solution works, why it stands out, and the real impact it will create over time.
